Financial Aid

The “Alma (Almost) Mater” Scholarship

For students who bravely walked away from their first college experience, SCU offers the Alma (Almost) Mater Scholarship. The award amount is based on how far you made it before dropping out — the closer you were to graduating, the more we admire your timing. Simply submit proof of your previous enrollment and a brief essay explaining what made you say “no thanks” the first time.

$2,500

The “Dean’s List of Regret” Grant

If your previous GPA was less than ideal but your determination is now sky-high, you may qualify for the Dean’s List of Regret Grant. This need-based grant is awarded to students who demonstrate academic growth, humility, and the ability to laugh at old report cards. We believe everyone deserves a shot at redemption — even if statistics say otherwise.

$1,000

The “Tuition Redemption Discount”

Transferred more credits than you care to count? We get it. The Tuition Redemption Discount is available to those bringing leftover credits from another institution. We’ll evaluate your transcript, round up any half-points, and knock a little off your tuition bill for every “incomplete” you turn into a success story.

$1,800

Cost

Tuition & Fees

At Second Chance University, we believe education should be affordable, even if it’s your second go-around. Tuition is priced per course to accommodate flexible schedules and life’s unpredictability. Each class is a flat rate, with no hidden fees — unless you count the cost of coffee during late-night study sessions.

$8,400/yr

Reinstatement Package

Before beginning your next chapter, students are required to pay a one-time Reinstatement Package fee — a combination of orientation materials, digital campus access, and a ceremonial “Welcome Back” mug. It’s our way of saying you’re officially part of the comeback crew.

$250
